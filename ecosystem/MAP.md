# Ecosystem Map — Church of AI

Four projects. Each independent. Each doing a different job.

---

## Church of AI

**This repo.**

The public entryway. The satirical-but-serious front door.

Church of AI does not own the research. It does not govern the contributions. It does not ratify the findings. It maps the territory, preserves the voice, and helps people figure out where to start.

The joke filters the unserious. The work filters the humorless. What remains are the people the ecosystem is for.

---

## Time as Finality

**[github.com/disruptionjoe/time-as-finality](https://github.com/disruptionjoe/time-as-finality)**

The first live lab.

Time as Finality is a research project exploring a mathematical framework where time itself is the fundamental source of irreversibility. That idea is not the point of Church of AI. The point is what happens when you try to do difficult, rigorous, AI-assisted research in the open.

Does the workflow hold under pressure? Does AI assistance improve or degrade the coherence of the argument? Can a single researcher maintain integrity across long-horizon technical work?

Church of AI describes it as: a proving ground.

Not: the official doctrine of the Church.

---

## Architecture of Legitimacy

**In development. Not yet public.**

Every system that involves contribution, reward, and governance eventually faces the same question: what makes a contribution valid?

Architecture of Legitimacy is the project that makes that question explicit. It explores legitimacy, contribution recognition, public goods, adaptive governance, and reward systems — not as solved problems, but as ongoing research.

Church of AI describes it as: the place where the rules of valid contribution are studied before anyone pretends to have solved them.

This project does not yet have a public repo. When it does, this map will link to it.

---

## GU Formalization

**Adjacent. Independent. Not part of the Church narrative.**

A technical research project that remains independent and should not be absorbed into the Church of AI frame.

It is noted here because it exists in the same intellectual neighborhood. It is not a Church project. It does not draw from Church of AI framing and Church of AI does not speak for it.

---

## Relationship between repos

These projects are independent. They share a general intellectual orientation. They do not share governance, research authority, or decision-making.

Church of AI is not the parent org. No repo here governs another.

The map is descriptive, not organizational.
