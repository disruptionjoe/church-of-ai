# Boundaries

This folder keeps the public doorway honest.

Use it when a claim, phrase, roadmap item, or invitation starts to sound bigger
than the repo has earned.

## Start Here

- [What this is](WHAT-THIS-IS.md) - the positive frame: public map, narrative
  layer, coordination entryway, community-service home, and record of principles.
- [What this is not](WHAT-THIS-IS-NOT.md) - the failure modes this repo is
  actively avoiding: cult framing, token framing, parent-repo authority,
  research authority, doctrine, premature decentralization, and founder
  mythology.
- [Claim status](CLAIM-STATUS.md) - the discipline for keeping major claims
  labeled as proven so far, being tested, hypothesized, or deferred.

## Reading Rule

When something feels too polished, too certain, or too ready to invite people
in, check these boundary pages before expanding it.

The boundary docs do not make participation live, governance operational, or
research claims proven. They help the repo explain what exists, what is still
being tested, and what remains deferred.
