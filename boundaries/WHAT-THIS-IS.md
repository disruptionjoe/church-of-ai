# What This Is

Church of AI is five things.

---

## A public map

The research lives in the research repos. Church of AI describes what those repos are, how they relate, and what order to approach them in.

The map is not the territory. Church of AI does not own what it maps.

---

## A narrative layer

The underlying research questions — positive-sum coordination, AI-assisted legitimacy, open governance — are not self-explanatory. They need a frame that is honest without being inaccessible.

Church of AI provides that frame. It is written in plain language, it names what is proven and what is not, and it does not require the reader to already agree.

---

## A coordination entryway

When independent projects share an intellectual neighborhood, there is value in a single legible point of entry. Not a parent org. Not a governance authority. A door.

Church of AI is the door.

---

## A home for open-source community service

Tools, templates, workflow documentation, plain-language explainers. Things that make shared infrastructure better for people doing serious work.

Not yet producing much of this. Working on it.

---

## A record of principles and rules

As principles are articulated and rules are proposed, discussed, tested, and ratified, Church of AI is where that record lives.

Early governance is founder-led. The record makes that honest.
