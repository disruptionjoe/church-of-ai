## What is not here

No participation pathways.
No contribution guide.
No governance process.
No tools or templates yet.
No community.

These are deferred. The entryway is built. The rest has to earn its place.

---

## What comes next

Check [ROADMAP.md](../ROADMAP.md) for what V2 requires.

The honest short answer: at least one piece of community service output that others can actually use, and a governance proposal that is documented enough to be discussable.

Neither of those is ready yet.

---

## The commitment at launch

Start with what is true.

Not what sounds good. Not what performs legitimacy. What is actually true about the state of this project right now.

That commitment is either going to hold or it isn't. This update is the first test of it.
