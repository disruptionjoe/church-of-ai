# Update: Content Distribution Planning

*July 2026*

Snapshot note: this update records the admitted V1 distribution planning state.
It does not override the current state in [STATUS.md](../STATUS.md),
[ROADMAP.md](../ROADMAP.md), or the [boundary docs](../boundaries/README.md),
and it does not authorize account setup, posting, scheduling, participation, or
external publication.

---

## What happened

Church of AI added a content-only [distribution planning packet](../distribution/README.md).

The packet names channel fit and content rules for helping people find the
public map, updates, and reading sequence without making the project sound more
ready, participatory, governed, or externally active than it is.

---

## What is live

- Channel-fit planning for Substack and short-video mirrors.
- Rules for preserving claim status in future distribution content.
- A setup boundary that keeps account setup, posting, scheduling, and external
  publication outside agent authority.
- An accepted Church of AI / Drafting Factory interface for local inventory and
  ready-package production, while external account setup and posting remain
  Joe-only actions.

---

## What is not live

No accounts are being created.
No posts are being published or scheduled.
No participation pathway is open.
No governance process is operational.
No membership, rewards, DAO, token, or proving-ground invitation is implied.

The packet is planning, not activation.

---

## The useful next move

The next dependency is the production relationship and channel contract, not
channel selection by itself.

Until then, the public repo can point readers to what already exists: the
[ecosystem map](../ecosystem/MAP.md), the [reading sequence](../ecosystem/SEQUENCE.md),
the [status](../STATUS.md), and the [roadmap](../ROADMAP.md).
