# Open-Source Community Service

This section defines what community service means in the Church of AI ecosystem.

It is not a vague commitment to doing good.

It is a specific set of activities with a specific goal: making shared infrastructure better for people trying to do serious work.

---

## What counts as service here

- Building useful open-source tools
- Improving shared research workflows
- Writing plain-language maps that help people navigate complex ideas
- Creating templates others can reuse
- Helping people understand difficult ideas without watering them down
- Making cooperation easier
- Improving the rules in public

See [OPEN-SOURCE-COMMUNITY-SERVICE.md](OPEN-SOURCE-COMMUNITY-SERVICE.md) for the full treatment.

---

## What does not count

- Charity theater
- Content that performs helpfulness without providing it
- Tools built primarily to generate attention rather than solve problems
- "Community" that exists only in the framing, not in the work
