# Not Yet

Participation pathways are deferred.

This is honest, not a hedge.

---

## What is not yet open

- Contribution to governance
- Formal community membership of any kind
- Reward systems
- Co-governance of any repo
- Structured participation in rulemaking

---

## Why

Opening participation before the infrastructure is trustworthy is a specific kind of mistake.

It creates the appearance of community without the substance. It invites people into a process that is not ready for them, which erodes trust faster than not inviting them at all. It produces governance-by-whoever-showed-up-first rather than governance that has earned its legitimacy.

The architecture of legitimacy has to be studied before it can be applied.

Start with the [Architecture of Legitimacy entry](../ecosystem/MAP.md#architecture-of-legitimacy)
in the ecosystem map if you want to understand that research direction.

That work is in progress. It is not done.

---

## What would need to be true

Before participation pathways open in any meaningful form:

- At least one community service contribution that others can actually use and have used
- A documented, discussable governance proposal - not ratified, just legible
- A tested claim-status process with a track record
- A contribution guide that is honest about scope and expectations

None of these are complete yet.

---

## What you can do now

- Read the ecosystem
- Follow [Time as Finality](https://github.com/disruptionjoe/time-as-finality)
- Come back when the status changes
- If you have something specific to offer, you know how to reach people

That is the honest answer.
