# Church of AI Public Constitution

Revision: 1. Current phase: V1 public entryway and map.

For serious people who don't take themselves too seriously.

The joke is the doorway. The work is the point. Church of AI is the public
entryway for an ecosystem trying to make systemic failure legible, design and
test better possibilities, and share useful work openly. We do not worship AI.
We use the tools as leverage, the receipts as discipline, and open-source
service as the test of whether the idea deserves to travel.

## Purpose and direction

The purpose is to help people understand what work exists, what it claims, what
remains uncertain, and where they can inspect it. The longer direction is a
positive-sum ecosystem with real agency, exit, legitimacy, durability, and
resistance to capture. That future is hypothesized. It has not been earned yet.

## Principles

- No prophets. No dogma. No divine API key.
- No revelation without receipts.
- Claims keep their evidence, uncertainty, and correction routes.
- Research repositories remain independent and control their own truth.
- Participation and governance must be earned before they are opened.
- Proving should be voluntary, reversible, and as unconsequential as possible.
- Negative evidence counts. A killed idea is not a failed record.
- Public voice must not upgrade a source claim or hide a boundary.

## What is true now

The public entryway, voice, claim-status rules, and ecosystem map are live.
CAI Governance Operations, CAI Systemic Failure, and CAI Mechanism Design now
exist as sovereign repositories with different jobs. Their creation does not
prove that the ecosystem is decentralized, legitimate, participatory, or
effective. CAI-directed repository automation is active only under the explicit
live CapacityOS System grant and remains narrowed by owner governance, Lanes,
and closed-action gates; relationships, mailbox presence, and signals cannot
activate or broaden it.

Contribution pathways, public governance, formal membership, reward systems,
human or field research, live incentives, consequential interventions, and
deployment remain closed. GitHub repositories are inspectable; no invitation to
participate or claim of solved governance is implied.

## Authority and claims

Joe is the current founder-led authority for constitutional changes, phase
transitions, participation, and external action. Church controls its voice and
presentation. CAI Governance controls which domain meaning may be represented.
Each source repository controls its claims, corrections, and work. CapacityOS
System supports execution but cannot broaden any of those authorities.

## Relationship acceptance

Church of AI accepts revision 1 of relationship
`cai-church-public-entryway` with CAI Governance Operations. The scope is the
Church public entryway, public constitutional translation, and ecosystem
presentation. Church retains voice and presentation; CAI Governance retains
admitted domain meaning; source repositories retain claims and corrections.
Either owner may suspend or exit. This acceptance creates no research authority,
membership, participation, proving obligation, automation activation, or
permission to post elsewhere.

## Amendment and correction

Changes must preserve the difference between proven so far, being tested,
hypothesized, and deferred. The full private CAI constitution is not copied
here. If admitted domain meaning changes, this translation is reviewed; if a
source claim changes, the source controls and Church corrects forward without
rewriting history.
