Status: active internal production interface, 2026-07-20.

Church of AI retains voice, claim-status, participation-boundary, and
distribution authority. Drafting Factory retains the sole production backlog,
prioritization, drafting, ready inventory, and local production learning. Joe
alone creates accounts, posts, schedules, or otherwise releases content.

## Initial working content jobs

The Factory may use these initial jobs immediately and refine them after the
next channel-job review:

1. Make one Church idea intelligible in a short, honest explanatory piece.
2. Give a reader an inspectable example of claim discipline, research status,
   or the public map.
3. Invite a safe next action: read the map, follow the work, or read an update.

No job authorizes a contribution invitation, membership claim, governance
promise, or external action.
