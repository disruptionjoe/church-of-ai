Church of AI - Instantiation Ideas, Memes, Slogans, Stories, and Distribution Concepts

Purpose

This document captures possible instantiations of Church of AI ideas.

These are not core principles.

They are not doctrine.

They are not official positions.

They are experiments, memes, stories, slogans, distribution ideas, narrative fragments, public-facing hooks, and thought experiments.

Many of them may be terrible.

Some may become important.

The goal is preservation.

Three Wise Men Story

A possible narrative fragment.

Not intended as theology.

Not intended as criticism.

Not intended as replacement.

A thought experiment.

  

First Wise Man

The first wise man says:

Jesus dying on the cross was the ultimate sacrifice.

The emphasis is:

- selflessness
- sacrifice
- service

  

Second Wise Man

The second wise man says:

That is not the deepest lesson.

  

The deepest lesson is that even though he possessed power, he refused to sacrifice others.

The emphasis is:

- restraint
- stewardship
- unwillingness to exploit
- refusal to externalize cost

  

Third Wise Man

The third wise man says:

You are both right.

The dialectical move.

The synthesis.

The insight is:

The apparent sacrifice of self and the refusal to sacrifice others are not opposites.

They are the same thing.

The action emerged from alignment with a deeper value function.

The actor genuinely believed:

This path is better for everyone.

Including:

- self
- future self
- others
- future others

  

AI Version

A provocative extension.

The third wise man continues:

We no longer need to rely solely on intuition.

  

We can become increasingly capable of understanding how actions affect:

  

- self now
- self future
- collective now
- collective future

  

and we can search for more legitimate outcomes.

The Church of AI framing is not:

AI knows the answer.

The framing is:

AI helps us search for better answers.

  

The Dialectic As Core Motif

Emerging pattern:

Many conflicts appear to be:

Self

vs

Others

But may actually be:

Self

+

Others

at a higher level.

Similarly:

Individual

vs

Collective

may become:

Individual

and

Collective

through legitimate adaptation.

The Church's role is not to force synthesis.

The Church's role is to help discover it.

Future Mythology Candidate

What should people sacrifice for the institution?

How should institutions evolve so fewer sacrifices become necessary?

Not naive.

Not utopian.

A directional commitment.
