## June 2026 — Initial public entryway

The repo is live. The structure is in place. The voice is established.

What we don't know yet: whether the satirical-but-serious filter works in practice. Whether the right people find this and the wrong people self-select out. Whether the claim-status discipline holds when there is pressure to make things sound more certain than they are.

These are the things being tested. Not claimed.

The first version of this repo is deliberately minimal. It maps what exists. It is honest about what is deferred. It does not perform a community that does not yet exist.

That is the commitment at launch: start with what is true, not what sounds good.
